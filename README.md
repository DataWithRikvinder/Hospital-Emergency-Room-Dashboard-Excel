# 🏥 Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard (Excel)

An interactive **Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard** built in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze patient visits, waiting time, admissions, referrals, patient satisfaction, and demographic trends. This project demonstrates the use of Excel's advanced data analytics features to transform raw healthcare data into actionable business insights
---
## 📌 Project Overview
The goal of this project is to provide hospital administrators and healthcare professionals with an easy-to-understand dashboard for monitoring Emergency Room (ER) performance. The dashboard enables users to analyze patient flow, identify operational trends, and support data-driven decision-making.
---
## 🎯 Business Objective
- Monitor Emergency Room performance.
- Analyze patient admission and attendance trends.
- Track average waiting time.
- Measure patient satisfaction.
- Identify referral patterns across departments.
- Understand patient demographics through age and gender analysis.

## 📌 Business Insights
- A total of **494 patients** visited the Emergency Room during the selected period.
- **53%** of patients were not admitted, while **47%** required hospital admission.
- The **average waiting time** was **36.39 minutes**.
- The **patient satisfaction score** averaged **5.06**.
- Attendance analysis showed **62% of patients arrived on time**, while **38% experienced delays**.
- The gender distribution was nearly balanced, with **49% female** and **51% male** patients.
- The majority of referrals were categorized as **"None"**, followed by **General Practice** and **Orthopedics**.
- Patients aged **10–19 years** represented the highest number of visits among all age groups.
